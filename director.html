Director's Message

Guiding our institution towards a brighter future

Jitendra Shukla

The Director, TPS

A Vision for Excellence

Dear Parents and Students,

Welcome to Time Public School. It gives me immense pleasure to introduce you to our institution, which has been established with a vision to provide quality education and foster the holistic development of every child.

In today's rapidly changing world, education is not just about academic excellence; it is about building character, nurturing creativity, and instilling values that will guide our students throughout their lives. At Time Public School, we strive to create a learning environment that is stimulating, safe, and inclusive.

Our dedicated team of educators is committed to unlocking the potential of every student. We employ modern teaching methodologies, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and a comprehensive curriculum to ensure that our students are well-prepared to meet the challenges of the 21st century.

We believe in the active partnership between the school and parents. Your involvement and support are crucial to the success of our students. Together, we can create a nurturing ecosystem that encourages our children to dream big and achieve their goals.

"I invite you to explore our campus and experience the vibrant learning community at Time Public School. Let us join hands to shape a bright and successful future for your child."

Warm Regards,

Jitendra Shukla

Director, Time Public School

Time Public School is committed to providing a nurturing environment where students are empowered to achieve academic excellence and holistic growth. Join us in shaping the leaders of tomorrow.
